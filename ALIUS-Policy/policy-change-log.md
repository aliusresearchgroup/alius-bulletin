# ALIUS Policy Change Log

This log summarizes substantive policy and internal-rule changes represented in the archived LaTeX sources. It is an orientation aid, not a substitute for the source files.

## Changes

### Undated Baseline - Internal Rules

- Status: Clean baseline snapshot, exact approval date unknown.
- Source file: [versions/undated-baseline-internal-rules.tex](versions/undated-baseline-internal-rules.tex)
- Summary: Establishes the baseline internal rules for membership categories, coordinators, rule modification, new memberships, membership renewal, diversity, and exclusion.

### 2022-04 - April 2022 Membership Revisions

- Status: Clean dated snapshot plus tracked-change source.
- Clean source: [versions/2022-04-internal-rules.tex](versions/2022-04-internal-rules.tex)
- Change source: [changes/2022-04-changes-from-baseline.tex](changes/2022-04-changes-from-baseline.tex)
- Summary: Removes the junior research member category, leaving research members and non-research members as the two member types.
- Summary: Removes the PhD/equivalent requirement and the explicit peer-reviewed-publication requirement from the research-member inclusion criteria.
- Summary: Updates voting language so research members and non-research members take part in new-member votes.

### 2022-10 - October 2022 Governance and Renewal Revisions

- Status: Tracked-change source; corresponding clean dated snapshot not present in the supplied zip set.
- Change source: [changes/2022-10-changes-from-2022-04.tex](changes/2022-10-changes-from-2022-04.tex)
- Summary: Removes the maximum of three non-research members.
- Summary: Changes rule-amendment voting from once a year to twice a year.
- Summary: Removes the coordinator duty to update the website publication list at least once every three years.
- Summary: Simplifies renewal/activity expectations toward ALIUS Bulletin participation and future contribution plans.
- Summary: Adds an extension mechanism when research activities are interrupted, such as maternity leave.

### 2024-05 - May 2024 Proposed/Status-Unknown Revisions

- Status: Proposal or tracked-change source; approval status unknown from the supplied file.
- Change source: [changes/2024-05-proposed-changes.tex](changes/2024-05-proposed-changes.tex)
- Summary: Renames non-research members as active role members.
- Summary: Replaces the fixed gender-diversity threshold with an Inclusion Officer role that reports annually with diversity and inclusion recommendations.
- Summary: Adds a tiered annual membership fee commitment with an option for a nominal contribution.
- Summary: Changes failed renewal language from membership not being renewed to membership being deactivated when no extenuating circumstance is communicated.
- Summary: Adds a pathway for deactivated members to reactivate without reapplying.

### 2025-10 - October 2025 Clean Snapshot

- Status: Latest clean dated snapshot in the supplied zip set; relationship to the May 2024 proposal is not assumed.
- Source file: [versions/2025-10-internal-rules.tex](versions/2025-10-internal-rules.tex)
- Summary: Provides a clean October 2025 version using research members and non-research members, with the October 2022 simplifications reflected in the text.
